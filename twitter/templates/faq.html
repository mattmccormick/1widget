Payment

How Do I Pay?

While logged into your account, go to Account -> Upgrade Package. Then click on the Subscribe button for the package you would like to subscribe to.

I have some concerns about paying...

Payment is handled through Paypal - one of the leaders in Online Payment Processing. All credit card/bank information is sent through Paypal. We do not have access to any of your credit card numbers or other financial information. In addition, you will only be billed monthly and can cancel anytime before the next monthly payment.

Digests

What is a Digest?

A Digest is a collection of RSS and/or Twitter Feeds. You can place multiple feeds into one Digest and then include the Digest on your website or blog.

For example, if you run a blog about Cars, you may want to add a few relevant Twitter users and a few Car RSS feeds to one digest. They will all be included but only the most recent news items will appear on your site.

Twitter

Why Do I Need to Give You My Twitter Account Authorization Before Adding Twitter Feeds?

We only ask for read access to your account. We cannot post or change anything on your account. We ask for authorization because Twitter currently only allows 3rd party websites to make 150 requests per hour to their service. If we didn't ask for Twitter authorisation, we could only get 150 Twitter feeds per hour. We would quickly exhaust this limit with many users wanting to add Twitter feeds to their digest.

If we make requests from each user, then we can get up to 150 feeds per hour per user.

To learn more about Twitter's Rate Limiting see http://apiwiki.twitter.com/Rate-limiting
